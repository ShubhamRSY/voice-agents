What Nexus delivers

Four pillars of modern customer experience — explained clearly, no mock chat windows.

💬

AI Chat with grounded answers

Real-time streaming conversations powered by your knowledge base — every reply can cite the exact source your team trusts.

  • SSE & WebSocket streaming for instant responses
  • RAG citations link back to policy docs and KB articles
  • Full session history and handoff to human agents
  • Embeddable widget for web and mobile apps
🤖

Agent Copilot — assist in the moment

Give frontline agents the right words, context, and next steps while the customer is still on the line.

  • Draft replies from live transcripts in seconds
  • Summarize long threads before escalation
  • Suggest next-best actions from CRM context
  • Tone-aware responses that match your brand voice
📞

Voice on real phone lines

Customers call your number — Nexus listens, understands, and speaks back with the same intelligence as chat.

  • PSTN via Twilio, Amazon Connect, or SIP/CCaaS
  • Live speech-to-text and neural text-to-speech
  • IVR flows, warm transfer, and voicemail capture
  • Same orchestrator and guardrails as digital channels
🔌
