Acme Corp Product Knowledge Base

## Billing & Payments

Q: How do I update my payment method?
A: Log into your Acme account at portal.acme.com, navigate to Settings > Billing, and click "Update Payment Method". You can add a credit card, debit card, or link a bank account via ACH. Changes take effect immediately for future charges.

Q: When am I billed?
A: Acme bills on the 1st of each month for the upcoming billing period. Annual plans are billed once per year on your subscription anniversary date. You will receive an email receipt within 24 hours of each charge.

Q: How do I get a refund?
A: Refunds are available within 30 days of purchase for annual plans. Contact support with your order number. Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable but can be cancelled anytime to prevent future charges.

## Account Management

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Visit portal.acme.com/reset and enter your email address. You will receive a reset link valid for 1 hour. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

Q: How do I add team members?
A: Go to Settings > Team > Invite Members. Enter their email addresses and select a role (Admin, Editor, or Viewer). Invited members receive an email with a link to join your workspace.

Q: What are the plan limits?
A: Starter plan: 5 users, 10GB storage. Professional plan: 25 users, 100GB storage. Enterprise plan: unlimited users, 1TB storage with custom limits available.

## Technical Support

Q: What browsers are supported?
A: Acme supports the latest two versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Internet Explorer is not supported. For mobile, use our iOS app (14+) or Android app (10+).

Q: Is there an API?
A: Yes. Acme provides a REST API with OAuth 2.0 authentication. API documentation is available at docs.acme.com/api. Rate limits: Starter 100 req/min, Professional 1000 req/min, Enterprise custom.

Q: How do I export my data?
A: Navigate to Settings > Data > Export. You can export as CSV, JSON, or PDF. Large exports are processed asynchronously and delivered via email within 24 hours.

## Troubleshooting

Q: I'm getting a 403 error when accessing the API.
A: A 403 error typically means your API key lacks the required scope. Verify your key has the correct permissions in Settings > API Keys. Also check that your account is in good standing.

Q: The app is running slowly.
A: First, clear your browser cache and cookies. Check your internet connection. If issues persist, try disabling browser extensions. For persistent slowness, contact support with your account ID and browser version.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: Email support@acme.com, call 1-800-ACME-HELP (available Mon-Fri 8am-8pm ET), or use the in-app chat. Premium and Enterprise customers have 24/7 phone support.
